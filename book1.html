Ram C/o Anandhi

Author: Akhil P. Dharmajan

Genre: Novel, Romance, Drama

Language: Malayalam (Available in English)

Synopsis

The story revolves around Ram, who moves from Alleppey, Kerala, to Chennai with aspirations of learning filmmaking and writing a novel. His life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters Anandhi, a receptionist at his college. The narrative explores their evolving relationship, filled with love, conflict, and the challenges they face. It also contains elements of revenge, and friendship. The book is set against the backdrop of college life and societal complexities.

Themes

The book delves into themes of love, revenge, loss, and friendship.

Author Background

Akhil P. Dharmajan is a bestselling novelist and scriptwriter. He has also worked as a screenplay writer in the Malayalam film industry, notably for the movie "2018."

About story

"Ram C/o Anandhi," penned by Akhil P. Dharmajan, is a contemporary novel that weaves together threads of romance, drama, and suspense, set against the vibrant and often challenging backdrop of Chennai. The narrative centers on Ram, a young man from Alleppey, Kerala, who arrives in Chennai with dual aspirations: to immerse himself in the world of filmmaking and to craft his own novel. Ram's journey in Chennai is not merely a pursuit of artistic dreams; it's a transformative experience that profoundly alters his life. His path intersects with Anandhi, a college receptionist, and their encounter sparks a relationship that forms the core of the story. Anandhi becomes a pivotal figure in Ram's life, and their connection is portrayed with a nuanced exploration of love's complexities. The novel delves into the intricacies of their relationship, portraying its evolution through moments of tenderness, conflict, and emotional depth. Dharmajan skillfully captures the nuances of their interactions, highlighting the emotional turbulence and the challenges they face as their bond deepens. Beyond the romantic narrative, "Ram C/o Anandhi" explores themes of friendship and revenge. Ram's experiences in Chennai are shaped by the people he encounters, and the dynamics of these relationships add layers of complexity to the storyline. The theme of revenge introduces an element of suspense, adding a darker dimension to the narrative and propelling the plot forward. The setting of the novel—college life in Chennai—provides a rich and dynamic backdrop. Dharmajan captures the essence of this environment, portraying the youthful energy, the social dynamics, and the challenges faced by young people navigating their way through education and personal growth. The societal complexities of Chennai also play a role in shaping the characters' experiences, adding a layer of realism to the story.

Author Background (Madhavikutty/Kamala Das)

Madhavikutty (Kamala Das) (1934-2009) was an Indian English poet and Malayalam author. She was known for her bold and honest writing on female sexuality and personal experiences. Her works are considered groundbreaking in Indian literature. She received numerous literary awards, including the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award and the Sahitya Akademi Award.

About the Story

"Atte Kadha" is Madhavikutty's candid and controversial autobiography. It details her personal experiences, including her childhood, marriage, and struggles with societal expectations. The book is known for its frank portrayal of female sexuality and its challenge to traditional norms. It's a significant work in Indian literature, offering a unique perspective on the life of a woman and an artist. The autobiography is known for its poetic language and its raw emotional honesty.

Author Background (M.T. Vasudevan Nair)

M.T. Vasudevan Nair (M.T.) (born 1933) is a renowned Malayalam author, screenwriter, and film director. He is considered one of the most influential figures in Malayalam literature. His works are known for their realistic portrayal of social issues and human emotions. He has received numerous literary awards, including the Jnanpith Award and the Sahitya Akademi Award.

About the Story

"Nilavinte Verukal" is a significant novel by M.T. Vasudevan Nair that portrays the impact of social change on rural Kerala. The story delves into the lives of individuals and families as they navigate changing social and economic landscapes. M.T.'s narrative is characterized by its evocative portrayal of rural life and its nuanced exploration of human relationships. The novel is known for its realistic characters and its insightful commentary on social issues. It captures the essence of a particular era in Kerala's history and its impact on the lives of ordinary people.
